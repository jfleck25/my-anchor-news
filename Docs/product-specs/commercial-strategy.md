# Commercial Strategy: The "Wedge"

**Date:** January 20, 2026  
**Status:** Active

## Strategy Overview

We will not compete with mass-market news immediately. We will attack a high-value niche first.

## Target Audience (The ICPs)

### Primary: "Briefing Ben" (Finance/Strategy)
- **Profile:** High income ($150k+)
- **Needs:** Signal, risk detection, and zero fluff
- **Pain Points:** Information overload, missing critical updates, time constraints
- **Use Case:** Morning briefing before market open, risk flag detection

### Secondary: "Drive-Time Dave" (Commuters)
- **Profile:** Daily commuters, 30-60 min drives
- **Needs:** Entertainment and friction-free audio during traffic
- **Pain Points:** Wasted commute time, boring podcasts
- **Use Case:** Hands-free news consumption during commute

### Tertiary: "Political Pam" (Niche)
- **Profile:** Political enthusiasts, early adopters
- **Needs:** Hyper-specific keyword tracking across obscure sources
- **Pain Points:** Missing niche news, tracking multiple sources manually
- **Use Case:** Keyword watchlists for specific topics/policies

## Pricing Model

### Free Tier ("The Headlines")
- **Limits:** 3 sources max, 12h history, Standard voice
- **Purpose:** Lead magnet
- **Features:**
  - Basic briefing generation
  - Standard persona voices
  - Shareable links

### Pro Tier ("The Analyst") - $20/mo
- **Limits:** Unlimited sources, 7-day history
- **Purpose:** Monetization engine
- **Features:**
  - Red Flag Mode
  - Executive Summary Export (PDF/Clipboard)
  - Priority source weighting
  - Advanced watchlists
  - All persona voices

### Enterprise ("The Newsroom")
- **Pricing:** Custom pricing for teams
- **Features:**
  - Calendar integration
  - Team sharing
  - Custom branding
  - Priority support
  - API access

## Go-to-Market Strategy

### Phase 6: Direct Outreach
- DM 50 finance pros on LinkedIn/Twitter
- Message: "I built an AI risk analyst that scans WSJ/Axios for you. Want a beta invite?"
- Focus: "Briefing Ben" persona

### Phase 7: Product-Led Growth
- Encourage "Briefing Ben" to share with "Drive-Time Dave" friends
- Short-form video marketing (TikTok/Reels)
- Social proof: Audio samples comparing personas

### Phase 8: B2B Sales
- Pitch Sales Directors: "We automate meeting prep for your driving reps"
- Newsletter sponsorships in aggregated Substacks
- Partnership opportunities for 24/7 AI News Feed

### Risk Mitigation Pitch
